About RPA

  • What is RPA ?

    Digital transformation has become one of the major focus areas of the businesses in recent times. It is the process of using digital technologies to create new or modify existing business processes, culture, and customer experiences to meet changing business and market requirements. In the race to outpace digital transformation, Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is often heralded as a quick and easy way to streamline critical processes, often extending the life of legacy systems.

  • RPA provides the human workforce the chance to use their ingenuity, creativity, empathy, and collaboration. The chance to bring their unique human value to the fore.

  • NASSCOM FutureSkills collaborates with Automation Anywhere to reskill professionals as well as the students. Both the organizations plan to build together a blended learning program with hands-on exposure to the cutting-edge Robotic Process Automation tool for the students in India.

  • Every student will receive access to Automation Anywhere A2019 Community Edition to work on industry’s first cloud-native RPA to develop the bots.

  • Examly supports National Botathon 2020 by providing the online platform for the participants.

    Any student undergoing Under-Graduate / Post-Graduate Degree / Diploma Program from any Institute in India. Students from any stream of Engineering, Non-Engineering Degree colleges, Diploma , Business Schools can join the program.

    Yes, you can join.

  • I am a student from Non-Engineering stream. Can I still join?

    Yes, you can join.

  • Is there an entry fee?

    No, there is no fee or payment

  • Shall I get Academic Credits for joining Botathon 2020?

    The competition has 45 to 60 Hrs of Practical components with mentor support and Certificate at the end of the completion – Qualifies for 1 to 1.5 Credit as per AICTE guidelines.

    Yes, every student upon successfully completing Bot Aspirant A2019 e learning course will receive a digital badge from Automation Anywhere, which will be available in your achievement dashboard on Automation Anywhere University portal.
